Privacy Policy

Last updated July 12, 2026

The short version

Running Calc does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There is no account, no analytics, no network access, and no third-party tracking of any kind. Everything you enter stays on your device.

What the app stores

The app saves a small amount of data locally on your device only, using Apple's standard on-device storage (UserDefaults):

  • Your saved Favorites (pace, time, and distance combinations you choose to save)
  • Your Appearance and Accent Color preferences

None of this data ever leaves your device. It is not sent to us, to Apple, or to any other party. Deleting the app deletes this data.

What the app does not do

  • No account creation or sign-in
  • No internet or network access of any kind
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or usage tracking
  • No advertising or ad tracking
  • No access to location, contacts, photos, or health data
  • No data sharing with third parties, because no data is collected to share

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes — for example, if a future version of the app adds a feature that uses data differently — this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will reflect that.
